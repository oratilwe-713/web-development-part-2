About M flavor fast food restaurant

M flavor is a local fast-food restaurant located in Limpopo, Polokwane CBD, 51 church str. M flavor sells a variety of wonder dishes like bunny chows, wings & chips, South African dishes like pap & chicken, pork, and beef.
