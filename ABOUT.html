About M Flavor Fast Food Restaurant

M Flavor is a local fast-food restaurant located in Limpopo, Polokwane CBD, 51 Church Street. We sell a variety of delicious dishes including bunny chows, wings & chips, and South African favorites like pap with chicken, pork, or beef.
